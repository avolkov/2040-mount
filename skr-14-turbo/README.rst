 
 
Overview
========

MKS GEN 1.4 mount to 2040 rail, and some wire management and daughterboard attachments.

WIP.


Hardware Required
=================

* M5 Bolts and M5 T-nuts for attaching to 2040 rail
* M3 bolts & nuts for attaching everything else
